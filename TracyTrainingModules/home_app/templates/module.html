Module # 1

Currently there are three ways to access ultratime:

The First Method:
1) Go to your myBerea login page
2) On the right side of the screen, under ‘My Single Signon Partner Applications’ click on the link that says ‘Ultratime Supervisor Functions’
3) Now login in with your credentials. Note: You won’t be redirected to ultratime until you login.
You will now be redirected to UltraTime.

The Second Method:
1) Go to your myBerea login page and login with your credentials.
2) Click on the ‘Work’ tab and under ‘Student Supervisor Links’ click ‘Student Time and Schedule’
You will now be redirected to UltraTime.

The Third Method:
1) For supervisors who do not have access to the myBerea portal, simply head to this URL: https://timemachine1-vm.berea.edu/UltraTime/
2) Login in with the username and password given to you by the system administrator.
You will now be redirected to UltraTime.
